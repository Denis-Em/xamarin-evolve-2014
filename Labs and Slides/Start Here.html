Evolve 2014 Training Workshops

Welcome

These lab exercises are intended to act as a guided set of projects for working with Xamarin technologies. You can select any of the below tracks to see the exercises available, and there are lab assistants available if you need any help or have questions.

Not every session has an associated lab, so the workshop is a place for you to explore your own ideas with Xamarin - work on your own code or projects and get advice and help from our instructor staff present in the room.
